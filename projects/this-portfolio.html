This Portfolio

Hand-coded from scratch — no frameworks, no templates, no drag-and-drop builders. Designed to feel product-y and intentional, with a visual identity rooted in a Polynesian perspective on carving space in tech.

Status Live
Stack HTML, CSS, Vanilla JavaScript
Role Solo — design & code

Why build from scratch

The honest answer: I want to work in tech, and using a template to represent myself felt wrong. A portfolio is a live artifact — it should reflect what you can actually do, not what Squarespace can do for you.

Building it by hand also forced me to care about the things I might otherwise skip: scroll behavior, responsive breakpoints, load performance, and what it actually means for a page to feel fast.

Design decisions

  • Dark background with deep green accents — a product aesthetic that doesn't shout
  • Sora for display type (geometric, clean, modern) paired with Inter for body copy
  • Scroll-triggered fade-up animations via IntersectionObserver, not scroll listeners
  • Radial gradient lighting in the hero to add depth without being loud about it
  • Card hover states with subtle lift and border brightening — tactile without being flashy

What it represents

Beyond the code, this portfolio is a stake in the ground. I'm Polynesian, I'm from Tacoma, and I'm building toward big tech on my own terms — not waiting for permission or a perfect resume. This site is part of that story.
